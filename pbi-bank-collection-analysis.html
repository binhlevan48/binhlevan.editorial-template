Bank Collection Analysis

1. Design Thinking

Step 1: Empahthize

1. Understand problem & Observe

What problem is the stakeholder trying to address? What difficulties are they facing?
- Understanding the repayment behavior of borrowers, analyzing the effectiveness of debt collection strategies

Why is it necessary to build this dashboard?
- Provide insights into loan collection activities, repayment trends, and portfolio health.

What is the goal of this dashboard?
- The goal of the dashboard is to help the stakeholder make data-driven decisions related to debt recovery strategies, and loan portfolio management.

Who are the users of this report?
- Collection managers, risk analysts, and executives responsible for loan recovery
