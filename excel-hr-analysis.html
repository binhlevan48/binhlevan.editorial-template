HR Attrition Analysis

1. Design Thinking

Step 1: Empahthize

1. Understand problem & Observe

What problem is the stakeholder trying to address? What difficulties are they facing?
- The stakeholder is trying to address attrition within the organization.
- Difficulties in understanding the factors contributing to attrition, identifying key patterns or trends, and identifying potential areas for intervention or improvement.

Why is it necessary to build this dashboard? What is the goal of this dashboard?
- Provide a comprehensive view of the employee attrition situation, identify underlying factors, and enable data-driven decision-making

What is the goal of this dashboard?
- Insights into attrition patterns, understand the impact of various factors, and aid in the development of strategies to reduce attrition rates.

Who are the users of this report?
- HR managers, department heads, and organizational leaders who are responsible for managing employee retention and making informed decisions to address attrition.
