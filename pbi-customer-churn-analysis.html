Customer Churn Analysis

1. Design Thinking

Step 1: Empahthize

1. Understand problem & Observe

What problem is the stakeholder trying to address? What difficulties are they facing?
- Address the issue of customer churn.
- Understanding the factors that contribute to customer churn, identifying patterns or trends that lead to churn, and developing strategies to reduce churn and retain customers.

Why is it necessary to build this dashboard?
- Provide insights into customer churn and help the stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

What is the goal of this dashboard?
- Enable the stakeholder to monitor churn rates, analyze customer behavior and characteristics, identify key churn drivers, and implement targeted retention strategies.

Who are the users of this report?
- Marketing analysts, and executives responsible for customer relationship management and business growth.
